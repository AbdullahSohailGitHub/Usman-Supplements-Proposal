Direct Answers

What you asked for, addressed directly.

01 / GemPages Experience
Advanced GemPages builds
I work extensively inside GemPages — custom sections, dynamic product blocks, conditional layouts, and reusable global elements — not just the drag-and-drop templates. If your page needs something the builder doesn't do out of the box, I extend it with custom code.
02 / Results Philosophy
How I measure a page
I won't hand you invented numbers — every store's baseline is different. What I will do is build against clear, measurable targets from day one: conversion rate, add-to-cart rate, and AOV, tracked before and after launch so we both know exactly what moved and why.
03 / Build Approach
Built to your spec, not a template
I start by studying your exact copy and page structure — no generic layout dropped in and hoped for. The page is built around your offer, your product story, and your customer, with the offer → trust → benefits → CTA → upsell structure as the backbone.
04 / Speed & Ongoing Work
Fast, precise, and available long-term
Mobile-first, lightweight sections, optimized images and scripts — speed is a conversion factor, not an afterthought. And if this page performs, I'm ready to keep building: new drops, seasonal campaigns, and ongoing CRO work.
The Process

From your copy to a live, optimized page.

2
